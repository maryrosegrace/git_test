Typeset Choices

There are many diffrent typeset choices and they can be used to create many diffrent website feels.

For example you can place emaphsis on a word by making the word bold. alternativly instead of making a word bold, you can put the word in italics.

It is interesting to note that in orer to make a word bold the tag words is strong, and to use italics the tag word is not a word at all, but only the letters e and m.
